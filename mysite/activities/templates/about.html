RunTrack is a simple app to log and track your running activities, designed for all users. Record your distance, duration, location, and we’ll track your progress to see how far you've come. Whether you’re training for a race or just enjoying a casual jog, RunTrack keeps every step of your journey in one place.

Get your running on track!

  1. Create an Account
    • Choose a username (just use your name!)
    • Create a password
    • Login with your credentials
  2. Log your running activity
    • Click the New Activity button
    • Enter the details of your run
    • Click the Save button
  3. Track your progress
    • View your activities on the Training Log page
    • Scroll through your feed of past run activities
    • View your activity stats over time

Logging a new activity

There are a variety of fields to edit when creating a new activity.
  1. Distance
    • Enter the distance, in miles, of your run.
  2. Duration
    • This is the duration, in seconds, of the activity.
  3. Elevation
    • Enter the elevation gain, in feet, that you climbed during your run.
  4. Date
    • This is the date on which the activity occurred.
  5. Time
    • Enter the time of day that you started your run.
  6. Location
    • Enter the location where your activity took place.
  7. Title
    • Give your activity a title to allow you to remember the run.
  8. Description
    • This is for extended details about your run. Did you enjoy it? Was it a tough workout? Enter the details here to help track your progress over time.
